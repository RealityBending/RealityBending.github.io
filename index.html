Reality Bending League

Exploring the neuropsychology of reality and its distortions

We’re a small team led by Dominique Makowski within the Clinical Brain Lab in Singapore. We research reality perception, fake news, illusions, fiction, deception, self-control, and more, using EEG and physiological signals. We also develop open-source tools and software.
