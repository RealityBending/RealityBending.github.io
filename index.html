Reality Bending Lab

Exploring the neuropsychology of reality and its distortions

We’re a small team led by Dominique Makowski within the Clinical Brain Lab in Singapore. We research reality perception, fake news, illusions, fiction, deception, self-control, and more, using EEG and physiological signals. We also develop open-source tools and software.

Research Themes

Reality bending

What are the mechanisms by which our belief and feeling of reality can be altered? What are the consequences?

Core Self

How do cognitive abilities unfold into personality traits and metastable neuropsychological profiles?

Metascience

What is the history of psychology and psychiatry? What are the methods and tools of tomorrow?
