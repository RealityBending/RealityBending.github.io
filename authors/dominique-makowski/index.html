Trained as clinical neuropsychologist, CBT psychotherapist (and others), I am currently a lecturer at the University of Sussex, in which I lead the Reality Bending Team üßô (ReBeL). I’m fluent in French, English, Polish, Python, and currently learning Italian. My main figures of reference are Marcus Aurelius, Sisyphus and Yoda. My interests include scientific methodology, history of art and religion, and philosophy. My research focuses on aspects of reality bending (e.g., fiction, deception, fake news, illusions, and altered states of consciousness such as through meditation or immersion).
